what we're all about

Our goal is to bring innovative ideas to the Penn State campus. The focus lies on face-to-face and word-of-mouth marketing campaigns. Today’s students are jaded to traditional forms of advertisement. Amplified provides businesses with a unique opportunity to directly reach out to Penn State students via face-to-face marketing in on- and off-campus hotspots.

What Amplified can do for you

We customize our campaigns to resonate with students at Penn State. Our marketing strategies focus on face-to-face and word-of-mouth interaction to minimize costs and maximize exposure.

Jobs/Internships

Become part of the Amplified team!

Promoter

As a member of the Amplified promotional team, you will help promote clients at our various promotional events on campus and downtown. You’ll engage people to get them interested in the client’s brand and hand out promotional items. The pay is $8/hour and you get to work around your own schedule!

Social Media Intern

A social media guru at Amplified focuses on sustaining our clients' presence on campus after initial exposure. Making our clients as exciting at Penn State as they are to us is the main goal. A social media intern will also get a chance to work on Amplified's own social media pages with the help of the entire Amplified team!

Software Developement Intern

An Amplified software developer develops anything from web applications to mobile apps with the goal of increasing the company's efficiency. We give you a creative environment to help bring ideas to life and, other than coding experience, our only prerequisite is enthusiasm!

Marketing Intern

We are looking for people who have excellent communication skills and are outgoing, friendly, and organized. This position focuses on understanding the college mentality and how to effectively bring students the products/services they want.
