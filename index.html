what we're all about

Our goal is to bring innovative ideas to the Penn State campus. The focus lies on face-to-face and word-of-mouth marketing campaigns. Today’s students are jaded to traditional forms of advertisement. Amplified.us provides businesses with a unique opportunity to directly reach out to Penn State students via face-to-face marketing in on- and off-campus hotspots.

Careers

Become part of the Amplified team!

Promoter

As part of the Promotional Team you will work at our promotional events on campus and downtown. You’ll engage people to get them interested in the client’s brand and hand out promotional items. The pay is $8/hour and you get to work around your schedule!

Account Manager

An account manager focuses on client relations, ensuring that each client is satisfied with the level of service that Amplified provides. This entails providing analytics on promotions and projections for a campaign as well as seeking out potential new clients.

Mobile Developer

The mobile developer develops all mobile applications for Amplified. The mobile developer works hand-in-hand with the web developer in order to ensure the company’s website and web applications work well on mobile OS’s.

How to Apply

We are looking for people who have excellent communication skills and are outgoing, friendly, and organized. Most importantly, we are looking for people who will help create a fun work environment! If you are interested in a position, contact topher@amplified.us.
